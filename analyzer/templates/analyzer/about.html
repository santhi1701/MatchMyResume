Why MatchMyResume?

Because your resume deserves to reach real recruiters. Let's make it ATS-friendly.

How It Works

Step 1: Upload Resume

Upload your resume (PDF or DOCX). We extract skills, tools, and experience using NLP.

Step 2: Add Job Description

Paste the job description you’re targeting. Our engine pulls out expected keywords.

Step 3: AI Analysis

We use TF-IDF + Cosine Similarity to calculate match score based on keyword overlap.

Step 4: Get Feedback

See matched/missing skills, improvement suggestions, and a smart match score.
